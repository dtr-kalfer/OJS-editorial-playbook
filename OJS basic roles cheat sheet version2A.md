# OJS BASIC ROLES CHEAT SHEET 
**VERSION - 2A**

---

## 1. Editor
> “Controls the publication process”

### 📋 Submission Handling
* **View all submissions**
* **Assign submissions to:**
    * Self
    * Other editors

### 🔍 Review Management
* Select and invite reviewers
* Set deadlines and send reminders
* **Decision Authority:**
    * Accept
    * Reject
    * Request revisions
* *Editors have the final say on publication.*

### ⚙️ Workflow Control
* **Move submission through stages:** `Submission` → `Review` → `Copyediting` → `Production`

### 📧 Communication
* Send emails to authors and reviewers.
* Uses templates (fully editable before sending).

### ⚠️ Risks / Common Mistakes
* Forgetting to assign reviewers.
* Skipping steps (jumping stages too fast).
* Sending wrong decision emails.
* Not checking reviewer feedback properly.

> **Editor Summary:** "The Project Manager of each paper."

---

## 2. Reviewer
> “Evaluates the quality”

### 📥 What they receive
* Assigned manuscript
* Review request email

### 🛠️ Capabilities
* **Access Files:** Download submission and view metadata.
* **Submit Evaluation:**
    * Write comments for the **editor** vs. the **author**.
    * Fill structured review forms (ratings/criteria).
    * Upload files (annotated manuscripts or supporting notes).

### 💡 Recommendations
The reviewer provides one of the following:
1.  **Accept**
2.  **Minor revision**
3.  **Major revision**
4.  **Reject**

### 🚫 Limits
* **Cannot see:** Other reviewers or author identity (if blind review is active).
* **Cannot:** Publish the paper or edit the submission directly.

### ⚠️ Risks
* Vague feedback.
* Not submitting the review on time (delays the entire cycle).

> **Reviewer Summary:** "The Quality Filter."

---

## 3. Author
> “Creates and improves the research”

### 📤 Submission Control
* Upload manuscript files.
* **Enter Metadata:** Title, abstract, and contributor details.

### 🔄 Revision Handling
* Upload revised versions.
* Respond to reviewer comments.

### 📈 Track Status
* **Monitor progress:** *Under Review*, *Revisions Required*, or *Accepted*.

### 📧 Communication
* Receive official decisions and requests from the editorial team.

### 🚫 Limits
* Cannot assign reviewers.
* Cannot edit the workflow or self-publish.

### ⚠️ Risks
* **Incomplete metadata** (Very common).
* Uploading the wrong file versions.
* Ignoring specific revision instructions.

> **Author Summary:** "Content creator + responder."
