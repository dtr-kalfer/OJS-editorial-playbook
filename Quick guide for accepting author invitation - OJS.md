# 📘 Quick Guide: Accepting Your Author Invitation

Welcome! Follow these simple steps to accept your Author role in the system.

---

## 🔹 Step 1: Log in to the Journal Portal

Before opening the email invitation, make sure you are already logged in to the journal site.

Use your assigned username and password to log in.

> **Tip:** Logging in first helps avoid errors during the acceptance process.

---

## 🔹 Step 2: Open the Invitation Email

Check your inbox for an email invitation.

This message contains your link to join as an Author.

---

## 🔹 Step 3: Click the “Accept” Link

Inside the email, click the **Accept Invitation** link.

If you are already logged in, you will be automatically redirected to the journal portal.

---

## 🔹 Step 4: Confirm and Continue

You will be taken to the acceptance page.

Click:

**Accept and Continue to OJS Page**

---

## ✅ You’re All Set!

You now have access to your Author role and can start submitting your work.

---

## 💡 Troubleshooting

If the link does not work properly:

* Make sure you are logged in to the portal first
* Try opening the link in the same browser where you logged in
* Log in again and retry the link
