# 🔒 Security Reminder: Logging Out of Your Account

For your security, always log out of your account after using the journal system—especially when using a shared or public computer.

---

## 🔹 Why This Matters

If you stay logged in on a shared device, others may:

* Access your submissions or reviews
* Make changes using your account
* View confidential editorial or reviewer information

---

## 🔹 How to Log Out

1. Go to the top-right corner of the page
2. Click your username/profile
3. Select **Log Out**

---

## 🔹 Best Practices

* ✅ Always log out after each session
* ✅ Avoid saving passwords on public/shared computers
* ✅ Use a private/incognito browser window when possible
* ✅ Double-check that you are fully logged out before leaving

---

## 💡 Extra Tip

If you accidentally forgot to log out on another device, log in again and change your password to secure your account.

---

Staying mindful of these small steps helps protect both your account and the integrity of the journal workflow.
